Why Join?

In the face of looming federal cuts, service providers across Alameda County are organizing to protect the communities we serve. Too often, providers are forced into a reactive posture — scrambling to respond to policy changes while juggling crises within their own organizations.

The Alliance brings providers together to share resources and engage in coordinated, proactive advocacy. Together, we tackle shared issues such as contracting and procurement challenges, fair payment standards, preparation for federal budget cuts, and advocate for the effective allocation of Measure W funds, among other priorities.

Moments of uncertainty demand collective action. This is the time to organize.
